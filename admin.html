💡 Tips for You

📸 Upload Product Photos
Go to "My Products" → "Add New Product" and upload a clear photo of your item. Good photos help customers buy more!
💰 Edit Price Easily
In the product list, click the ✏️ button next to any price to change it quickly without opening the full form.
⭐ Mark as Featured
Featured products appear on the home page. Mark your best items as featured to show them first!
🆕 New Arrivals
Mark new items as "New Arrival" so they appear in the New Arrivals section and attract more customers.
